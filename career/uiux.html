Become a professionalUI/UX Designer

Master design principles, UX processes, and build a strong portfolio employers respect.

← Back to Career Paths
Year 01

🧭 Foundations (Absolute Beginner → Beginner)

  • Goal: Learn design fundamentals & UX basics
  • Skills: Color, typography, layout, UX principles, UI basics
  • Tools: Figma, Canva, Miro
  • Practice: Recreate app screens (Instagram, Spotify)
  • Resources: Coursera, YouTube, “Don’t Make Me Think”
Year 02

🧭 Intermediate (Beginner → Skilled Designer)

  • Goal: Master UX process & improve UI
  • Skills: Personas, wireframes, prototypes, testing
  • Tools: Figma Advanced, Notion, Maze
  • Projects: Food delivery app, website redesign
  • Extras: Daily UI challenges
Year 03

🧭 Advanced Level (Skilled → Professional)

  • Goal: Build real-world case studies
  • Skills: Usability testing, handoff, micro-interactions
  • Tools: Zeplin, Framer, ProtoPie
  • Projects: Freelance/internship case studies
  • Extras: Learn basic HTML/CSS
Year 04

🧭 Job Ready (Professional → Designer)

  • Goal: Portfolio + internships + interviews
  • Skills: Product thinking, storytelling, UX strategy
  • Projects: 5–6 polished case studies
  • Extras: Networking, meetups, mock interviews
